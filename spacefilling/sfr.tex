\begin{abstract}
    Hilbert curve is a space-filling curve that fills the unit square. Based on a construction of Hilbert curve $\leftidx{_4}{H}{_4}$ of Liu,
    we give the construction of Hilbert spiral which fills the whole Euclidean plane $E_2$, and a conterpart construction on hyperbolic plane $H_2$.
    By using the perpendicular and parallel relationship introduced by Hilbert spiral in $H_2$,
    we can define a special conformal flow and this leads us to a new proof of Riemann mapping theorem.
\end{abstract}
